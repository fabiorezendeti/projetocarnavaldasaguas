Däniel blog - logo design

  • Inkscape
  • Trimage

My friend Daniel wanted a minimalistic black and white logo for his blog. So I started experimenting with vector graphic design in Inkscape and finaly came up with an idea.

I've chosen a circle as a border for the logo to indicate a hidden eternality. Two opposed leaf pairs represent spice and controversy in his work and his multispectral view of the world.

The three gradually dissapearing stars symbolize the flow of his ideas, fading in and out in time and hard to reach, just like real stars. They highlight the high level of abstraction in Daniel's writing. At the end, the iconic pen describes the main purpose of his blog best.
